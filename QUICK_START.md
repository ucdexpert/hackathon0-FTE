## 🎯 Real Example: Customer Inquiry

**What happens when customer comments:**

1. Customer comments: "What are your prices?"
2. Facebook Watcher detects (5 min)
3. Creates file in `Needs_Action/`
4. Claude Code reads and suggests reply
5. Creates approval file in `Pending_Approval/`
6. **You approve** (move to `Approved/`)
7. AI posts reply to Facebook
8. AI creates customer in Odoo
9. AI creates invoice
10. **You approve** invoice email
11. AI sends invoice
12. Customer pays
13. Monday: CEO Briefing includes this sale!
